About Me

I am a Registered General Nurse, Accredited Play Therapist specialising in Sand play, I have a Diploma in Integrative Counselling and Psychotherapy and I have an MSc in Bereavement Studies.

Continuous Professional Development courses

  • Diploma in integrative Sandplay studies
  • Certificate in Transpersonal Alchemical Studies Sandplay Specialisation
  • Certificate in Art Therapy
  • 6 day course on Clay and Bodywork
  • Positive parenting training for trainers
  • Dance Movement Psychotherapy 60 hour course
  • Applied Suicide Intervention Skills Training (ASIST)
  • Specialist Workshop on Complicated Grief Therapy (CGT)
  • Post Grad Certificate in Environmental Art Therapy

Professional bodies registered with:

  • An Bord Altranais
  • Play Therapy Ireland
  • Irish Association of Counsellors and Psychotherapists

I adhere to the professional standards set out in their code of ethics.
